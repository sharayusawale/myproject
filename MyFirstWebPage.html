My First Web Page

Tourism is a social, cultural, and economic phenomenon that involves people traveling outside of their normal environment for personal or professional reasons. It can include activities like sightseeing and camping, and people who travel for fun are called "tourists". The places where tourists stay are sometimes called "resorts", and the places they visit are called "tourist destinations".
